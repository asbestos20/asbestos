2D IBR

Select a track with the buttons above. Refresh to change tracks.

I think times are consistent across different devices, but I can't guarantee it. This is just a pet project; don't take the times too competitively.

You are a point mass: your boat has no hitbox, and collisions are based on the black dot at the center of your boat.
